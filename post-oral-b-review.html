Oral-B iO Series 9 Review: Is It Worth the Hype?

By Dr. Sarah Chen February 28, 2026 8 min read
9.2
out of 10
★★★★★ — Highly Recommended

As a dentist who sees the consequences of poor brushing habits every single day, I take electric toothbrush technology seriously. When Oral-B sent us their latest iO Series 9 for review, I committed to using it exclusively for 30 days before sharing my honest professional opinion.

The bottom line? This is one of the best electric toothbrushes I've ever tested — and I've tested dozens. But it's not without its drawbacks. Let me break it all down.

Design & Build Quality

The first thing you notice is how premium the iO Series 9 feels in your hand. The matte finish, the satisfying magnetic charging stand, and the interactive color display all scream luxury. It's a far cry from the chunky electric toothbrushes of years past.

The handle has a subtle texture that provides excellent grip even with wet hands. The single button interface is intuitive — press to turn on/off, hold to cycle through 7 cleaning modes. The color display shows your selected mode, battery life, and even a smiley face when you've brushed for the recommended 2 minutes.

Cleaning Performance

This is where the iO Series 9 truly shines. The micro-vibration technology combined with the round brush head delivers an incredibly thorough clean. After using it for a week, my teeth felt noticeably smoother and cleaner than with my previous electric toothbrush.

The 7 cleaning modes offer good versatility:

  • Daily Clean — Your everyday mode, excellent for routine brushing
  • Intense Clean — More powerful oscillation for a deeper clean
  • Whiten — Polishing mode that gradually reduces surface stains
  • Gum Care — Gentle mode specifically designed for gum stimulation
  • Sensitive — Perfect for patients with sensitive teeth or gums
  • Super Sensitive — Even gentler for post-procedure care
  • Tongue Cleaning — Low-speed mode optimized for tongue surface

Smart Features

The AI-powered 3D tracking is genuinely impressive. Using the Oral-B app, the toothbrush maps your mouth into 16 zones and tracks whether you're giving each area adequate attention. After a month of use, I can confirm the tracking is remarkably accurate.

The pressure sensor is another standout feature. An LED ring on the handle glows green when you're applying the right pressure, and turns red when you're pushing too hard. As a dentist, I see enamel wear from aggressive brushing all the time — this feature alone could save patients from damage.

Battery Life

Oral-B claims about 2 weeks of battery life with twice-daily brushing, and that's been consistent with my experience. The magnetic charging dock is convenient and elegant, though I wish they included a travel case with a USB charging option.

Pros

  • Exceptional cleaning performance
  • Smart pressure sensor prevents enamel damage
  • AI-powered brushing guidance is genuinely useful
  • Premium build quality and design
  • 7 cleaning modes for different needs
  • Interactive display is fun and informative

Cons

  • Premium price point (~$300)
  • Replacement brush heads are expensive
  • Travel case doesn't include charger
  • App can feel overwhelming at first

The Dentist's Verdict

The Oral-B iO Series 9 represents the pinnacle of electric toothbrush technology right now. The cleaning performance is outstanding, and the smart features — particularly the pressure sensor and zone tracking — genuinely help improve brushing habits.

Is it worth the $300 price tag? If you're serious about your oral health and can afford it, absolutely. However, the Oral-B iO Series 5 offers about 80% of the experience at roughly half the price, making it the better value pick for most patients.

My recommendation: If you've been using a manual toothbrush, ANY electric toothbrush will be a massive upgrade. But if you want the very best and don't mind the premium, the iO Series 9 delivers.
